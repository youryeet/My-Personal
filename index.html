    • Biggest Tournaments

    Wimbledon



    Wimbledon is hosted in London. The courts that the players pay in are made out of grass. Usually tennis players want the Wimbledon cup the most out of the 4 main tournaments. It happens around June-July

    Australian Open



    The Australian Open happens around February every year. The longest match ever played there was 5 hours and 53 minutes.

    US Open



    The US Open happens around August-September. Dominic Thiem won the 2020 US Open.

    French Open



    The French Open takes place every year around May-June. Rafa Nadal won the French Open when he was 17 and now so far has 13 French Open titles. The courts are made of clay.
